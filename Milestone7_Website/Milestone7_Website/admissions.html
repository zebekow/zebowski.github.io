Recipients

Children who lose their hair as a result of a medical condition don’t feel like themselves and lose some of their confidence day-to-day. Our custom hair pieces, or hair replacement systems, are made to fit each individual child’s head perfectly and allow them to play outside and participate in the activities they love without having to worry about their hair. Find out how to apply for a custom hair piece, learn more about our hair replacement systems, and read about some of the children we’ve had the privilege of helping.
